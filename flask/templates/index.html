What is ConsBOL?

ConsBOL provides constraint-based reasoning about biopolymers. It provides a language which should be used to constrain how the intervals of bioploymers relate to each other, including their position and length. ConsBOL will be included within the newest version of SBOL and used in ShortBOL.

ConsBOL is developed and maintained by Turing Ate My Hamster LTD and Christopher P D Taylor in partnership with Newcastle University.
